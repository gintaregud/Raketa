Blue

Blue is the color of the sky and sea. It is often associated with depth and atability. It symbolizes trust, loyality, wisdom, confidence, inteligence, faith, truth, and heaven

Green

Green is the colour of nature. It Symbolizes growth, harmony, freshness, and fertility. Green has strong emotional correspondence with safety.

Red

Red is the color of fire and blood, so it is associated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire, and love.
